RNGTool.. but on Android!
About

RNGTool Android was initially made by the original RNGTool creator, NinjaCheetah, though because they weren't familiar with Kotlin or java, the project had quickly become abandoned, having only gotten the main number generator.

Fastforward a bit, and I'm looking for something to work on with my newfound Java knowledge, and it just so happens there was an opportunity to maintain RNGTool Android! Since then, I've nearly gotten it to parity with the mode selection, though lacking some other features.

Requirements & Download
Requirements

To use RNGTool Android, you will need:

  • An Android device running Android 8.0 or higher.

Please note that devices running WearOS are not officially supported, and will not display or function as intended.
